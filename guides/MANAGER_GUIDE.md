# Partner Hub — Manager Guide
## For Partner Agency Leads · TrustedNetworx

As a **Manager**, you oversee your entire agency's activity on the Partner Hub. You can see all your team's data, invite new team members, and manage your account settings.

---

## What You Can Do

### 1. See Your Agency's Pipeline

Go to `/opportunities`. You see every deal every team member in your agency is working — pipeline stage, estimated ACV, close dates.

**What your team sees:** Each rep sees only their own deals.

### 2. Manage Your Team

Go to **Settings → Team Members** (`/settings/users`):

- **Invite a team member:** Enter their work email and choose their role (Rep or Admin). They receive an invite email. Once they sign up, they're automatically added to your agency.
- **View your roster:** See every team member with their name, email, role, and status.
- **Key contacts:** Set your Billing, Support, and Legal contacts — these are who TrustedNetworx communicates with for invoices, escalations, and legal matters.
- **Deactivate:** Click Remove on a team member to disable their access. Their data stays in your account.

### 3. Access the Content Library

Go to `/library`. Browse sales decks, one-pagers, battle cards, and demo scripts organized by folder:

| Folder | What's inside |
|---|---|
| Co-Branded Collateral | Decks with your agency's branding |
| Pitch & Demo Resources | Scripts, elevator pitches, presentation templates |
| Compliance & Life-Safety | NFPA 72, fire-alarm, elevator line materials |

**How to share:** Click **Share** on any item. Your prospect gets a tracked link. When they open it, you'll get a notification.

### 4. Complete Training

Go to `/training`. Your team has access to:
- **9 video courses** covering product training, sales techniques, and technical setup
- **12 downloadable documents** including playbooks, battle cards, and installation guides
- **Certification tracks** (coming soon) with badges and rewards

Track your team's progress in the Documentation LMS (`/documentation`).

### 5. Use the CRM

Go to `/crm`. Your agency's CRM includes:

| Page | What it does |
|---|---|
| `/crm` | Dashboard — total leads, companies, contacts, won deals |
| `/crm/leads` | Lead table with status filters and search |
| `/crm/contacts` | People directory linked to companies |
| `/crm/companies` | Organization profiles with industry and lead counts |
| `/crm/forms` | Embeddable lead capture forms for your website |

As manager, you see **all** leads, contacts, and companies in your agency.

### 6. Track Deals

Go to `/opportunities`. Each deal moves through 7 stages:
1. Qualification
2. Discovery / Site Survey
3. Proposal
4. Contracting
5. Closed Won
6. Awaiting Install
7. Activated

Click any deal to see its detail page: stage stepper, ACV calculator, engagement analytics.

### 7. Customize Your Branding

Go to **Settings → Brand & Company** (`/settings/brand-kit`):
- Upload your logo (used on co-branded collateral)
- Update company info (website, phone, email)
- Set your partner profile: lines under management, team size, voice platforms, PSA tools, verticals served

---

## What You Cannot Do

- ❌ See other agencies' data (strictly isolated)
- ❌ Promote anyone to admin (only TrustedNetworx can)
- ❌ Access the Admin Panel (`/admin` — restricted)
- ❌ Change your company's registered domain

---

## Your Login

- **URL:** `https://hub.trustednetworx.com/login`
- **Email:** Your work email (as registered by your admin)
- **Role:** `manager`

If you forget your password, contact your TrustedNetworx channel manager or use the "Forgot password" link on the login page.

## Support

**Level 1:** Your agency provides first-line support to end customers.

**Level 2:** TrustedNetworx NOC handles escalated technical issues. Contact your channel manager for escalation procedures.

The AI Assistant (green button, bottom-right) can answer questions about products, pricing, and the platform at any time.
