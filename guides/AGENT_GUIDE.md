# Partner Hub — Agent Guide
## For Sales Reps & Agents · TrustedNetworx

Welcome to the Partner Hub. This is your toolkit for selling TrustedNetworx solutions — POTS replacement, Hosted PBX / UCaaS, SIP trunking, internet connectivity, wireless failover, and managed voice.

---

## Your Dashboard

When you sign in, your Home page (`/`) shows:

- **Personalized greeting** — "Good morning, [Your Name]"
- **Quick actions** — Register a Deal, Open Content Library, Complete a Lesson
- **Onboarding tracker** — Your progress through the 6-stage partner onboarding
- **Recent notifications** — When prospects view your shared content

Everything you see is **yours only** — your leads, your deals, your contacts.

---

## Day-to-Day Tasks

### Register a Deal

The **"+ Register Opportunity"** button is in the top bar on every page. Click it anytime.

1. Fill in: Company name (required), stage (defaults to Qualification), lines/seats, vertical, contact name, contact email, notes
2. Click **Register Opportunity**
3. Your deal appears in your pipeline at `/opportunities`

**Track it:** Go to `/opportunities`, click your deal, and advance it through stages as you progress.

### Find Sales Content

Go to `/library`. Three folders of ready-to-use materials:

- **Co-Branded Collateral** — Decks and one-pagers with your branding
- **Pitch & Demo Resources** — Scripts, elevator pitches, presentation templates
- **Compliance & Life-Safety** — NFPA 72, fire-alarm, elevator documentation

**Share with a prospect:** Click **Share** on any item. They get a link. When they open it, you'll be notified.

### Complete Your Training

Go to `/training`. Your onboarding includes:

| Resource | What you'll learn |
|---|---|
| **Video courses** (9 total) | Product demos, objection handling, pipeline building, technical setup |
| **Documents** (12 total) | Sales playbooks, battle cards, pricing sheets, installation guides |
| **Documentation LMS** | Structured 6-section course with progress tracking |

Start with "POTS Replacement — Product Overview" and work through the Product Training section.

### Manage Your Leads

Go to `/crm`. Your personal CRM:

- **Leads** (`/crm/leads`) — Every prospect you're working. Filter by status (New → Contacted → Qualified → Proposal → Won/Lost)
- **Contacts** (`/crm/contacts`) — People directory linked to their companies
- **Companies** (`/crm/companies`) — Organization profiles

All data is **yours alone** — no one else in your agency can see your leads.

### Track Your Pipeline

Go to `/opportunities`. Your deals move through 7 stages:

```
Qualification → Discovery → Proposal → Contracting → Won → Install → Activated
```

On any deal's detail page, use the **"Advance to [next stage]"** button to move it forward. The estimated ACV is calculated automatically from your lines × MRC × 12.

## Getting Help

### AI Assistant
Green button in the bottom-right corner of every page. Ask things like:
- "What's the MRC for POTS replacement?"
- "How do I handle the price objection?"
- "Walk me through a UCaaS demo"

### Your Manager
Your agency lead can see your deals and help with escalations, pricing approvals, and deal strategy.

### TrustedNetworx Support
For technical issues, your manager escalates to the TrustedNetworx NOC (Level 2 support). TrustedNetworx does **not** communicate directly with your end clients — you are the face of the service.

## Tips

1. **Bookmark `/library`** — it's your go-to for every customer meeting
2. **Share content before meetings** — prospects who view shared decks are warmer leads
3. **Keep your pipeline updated** — advance deals promptly so your manager can help at the right time
4. **Complete the onboarding tracker** — each stage unlocks new content and tools
5. **Use the AI Assistant** — it's trained on all product specs and sales materials
